Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

For the shortcake:

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 2/3 cup Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

For the Strawberries:

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries
  • 2-3 tablespoons Granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

For the whipped cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream (chilled)
  • 2 tablespoons owdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F(220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold cubbed butter to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips,cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk and vanilla extract. Gradually pour the milk mixture into the flour mixutre, stirring until just comnined. Be careful not to overmix
  5. Turn the dough out into a lightly floured surface. Gently pat the dough into a rectangle about 3/4 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter or the rim of a glass to cut out circles of dought. Place the dough circles onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them a few inches apart.
  6. Bake the shortcakes into the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until they are golden brown. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. While the shortcakes are cooling, prepare the strawberries. In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries granulated sugar, lemon slices. Toss gently to coat the strawberries in the sugar. Let them sit for about 15-20 minutes to allow the juices to release.
  8. To make the whipped cream, pour the chilled heavy cream into a mixing bowl. Add the podered sugar and the vanilla extract. Using an electric mixer, beat hte cream on high speed until stiff peaks form.
  9. To assemble the strawberry shortcakes, sliced the cooled shortcakes in half horizontally. Spoon a generous amount of macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of each shortcake. Top with a dollop of whipped cream, then place the other half of the shortcake on top.
  10. Garnish the strawberry shortcakes with additional whipped cream and fresh strawberries, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious taste of summer in every bite.
